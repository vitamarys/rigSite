Palm Jumeirah Overview

The man-made island of Palm Jumeirah is a significant landmark of the UAE, along with the iconic Burj Khalifa and the famous Burj Al Arab. The popular island area is distinguished by elite property, luxury hotels, as well as beautiful white sand beaches and an array of water activities.¬†

  • Shaped like a huge palm tree, Palm Jumeirah is the first of the artificial islands constructed off the coast of Dubai and one of the largest man-made islands worldwide.
  • The exceptional community in the shape of a palm tree includes a trunk and sixteen symmetrical fronds with a concentration of marvellous properties and 5-star hotels.
  • The amazing project of Palm Jumeirah extends 3 miles into the Arabian Gulf and occupies a total area of 560 hectares.

Why buy property on Palm Jumeirah

Property on Palm Jumeirah is an ideal choice for those who appreciate a luxurious lifestyle on the beachfront with breathtaking sea views.

Buying a property on Palm Jumeirah is also a profitable investment, as this area is in high demand among tourists. Over the past two years, the number of luxury real estate deals with an average value of more than AED 10M (USD 2.7M) has increased significantly. Investors can purchase an off-plan property on Palm Jumeirah and resell it at a higher price in the near future. The cost of real estate will only increase since there are fewer and fewer available plots left in the community for construction.

Direct access to the sea

One of the main advantages of purchasing a residence on a man-made island is the direct access to the sea.

Benefits

The vibrant community of Palm Jumeirah is well-known for its extensive infrastructure and exclusive attractions. The area is a popular destination among tourists around the world due to its unique beachfront lifestyle with easy access to exceptional amenities and all the necessary facilities.

#1

Schools

The community of Palm Jumeirah is a suitable place for families with children to live thanks to the availability of educational institutions. Redwood Montessori Nursery - Golden Mile 8 Palm and Jumeirah International Nursery are all in the area. The closest schools include iCademy Middle East and Dubai College, which can be reached in less than a 10-minute drive.

#2

Healthcare

The area of Palm Jumeirah offers a number of medical institutions. They are presented by Nucleus Clinic, Al Das Medical Clinic, Emirates Hospital Clinics - Palm Jumeirah and Dubai London Clinic - Nakheel Mall. There is also Beverly Hills Wellness & Aesthetics Clinic in the community, which specialises in cosmetic procedures.

#3

Transport

The area of Palm Jumeirah is located close to Al Majarrah St and one of the main highways in Dubai, Sheikh Zayed Road, which provides easy connectivity to other parts of the emirate. Travel time to both Dubai International Airport and Al Maktoum International Airport takes about a 30-minute drive, which will be convenient for frequent travellers.

The community provides several modes of public transport. The most popular form of public transport is the Palm Jumeirah Monorail, which connects Atlantis the Palm to the gateway station at the entrance of The Palm. The train runs every 15 minutes.

Residents and visitors to the area also can use free shuttle bus services, which offer transportation across Palm Jumeirah and neighbouring districts.¬†.

Entertainment

The exclusive destination of Palm Jumeirah provides a wide range of entertainment options.
