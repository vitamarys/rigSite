Why buy property on Palm Jumeirah?

Palm Jumeirah is an exclusive man-made island in the shape of a huge palm tree with fronds as its branches. The luxurious waterfront community features elevated seaside living with easy access to the white-sandy beaches.

The iconic area attracts a large number of tourists each year due to its numerous world-famous attractions.

A number of well-known landmarks such as The View at the Palm, Palm Jumeirah Corniche Area, Dive Atlantis and The Lost Chambers Aquarium are concentrated on this prestigious island.

Palm Jumeirah is an ideal destination for young people.

Palm Jumeirah is an ideal destination for young people who strive to live in a vibrant waterfront neighbourhood with access to a well-developed infrastructure. One of the most popular activities in the area is to sail around the island on a private yacht or speedboat. Families with children choose the iconic community due to the accessibility of all the necessary infrastructure, its numerous white-sandy beaches and the exclusive entertainment facilities. Residential complexes on Palm Jumeirah provide family-oriented amenities such as BBQ zones, swimming pools, leisure areas, children’s playgrounds, and much more.

The vibrant area of Palm Jumeirah is distinguished by a well-developed infrastructure.

 Some of Dubai's most luxurious resorts such as Atlantis, FIVE Palm Jumeirah Hotel, The Palm and One&Only The Palm are located in the exclusive neighbourhood. The large shopping and entertainment complex of The Pointe, which is stretched along the picturesque embankment, is ideally suited for shopping, leisure and entertainment. There are plenty of restaurants in the area as well, including 101 Dining Lounge, Palm Avenue, ZOR Restaurant and Nobu.

The seaside community of Palm Jumeirah is distinguished by its good transport accessibility.

The seaside community of Palm Jumeirah is distinguished by its good transport accessibility, which makes it easy to get to the mainland of Dubai by both car and public transport. The Palm Monorail runs through the entire island right down the middle. The close proximity to the main highway of the emirate; the Sheikh Zayed Road, provides easy access to the rest of the city by car.

Property types on Palm Jumeirah

#1

Studios, 1-6 bedroom apartments, penthouses, duplexes

The vibrant community of Palm Jumeirah provides a wide choice of luxurious real estate, presented by elegant studios and 1-6 bedroom apartments, with living areas ranging from a compact 312 sq. ft to a whopping 52,488 sq. ft. You can also buy elite penthouses with 2-7+ bedrooms with property sizes varying from 1,670 sq. ft to 10,000 sq. ft.

There are 1-6 bedroom duplexes with spacious sizes ranging from 1,607 sq. ft to 52,488 sq. ft.

More spacious property on Palm Jumeirah, the waterfront district offers extraordinary villas

#2

Extraordinary villas, townhouses

If you want to purchase a more spacious property on Palm Jumeirah, the waterfront district offers extraordinary villas with 2-7+ bedrooms from 3,801 sq. ft to 66,000 sq. ft. You can also buy 2-5 bedroom townhouses with living areas varying from 3,046 sq. ft to 3,659 sq. ft, available for sale on the exclusive island.

#3

Half floors, full floors, plot

Investors can purchase half floors with areas ranging from 3,733 sq. ft to 6,537 sq. ft, as well as full floors with sizes varying from 9,049 sq. ft to 24,360 sq. ft. You can also acquire a land plot on Palm Jumeirah in order to construct property to your own preference.

Sizes of land plots in the area range from 7,736 sq. ft to an enormous 280,000 sq. ft.
