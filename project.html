About the project

Como Residences for sale

The residential complex by Nakheel Properties, will provide its occupants with resort-like living and access to endless premium amenities. Units in the complex will offer 180 or 360-degree views of the Arabian Gulf, Palm Jumeirah, the Burj Al Arab and the famous Dubai skyline, including the Burj Khalifa. Como Residences is planned to be completed in Q3 2027. 

The upcoming 71-storey residential tower with 3 podium floors will be reminiscent of waves and will harness the beauty of nature. The total number of luxurious residences will be 76, and will include 2–6 bedroom apartments, 7-bedroom duplex apartments and a unique 5-bedroom penthouse with living areas ranging from 4,469 sq. ft to 21,949 sq. ft. Each premises will come with 3–6 parking spaces, while residents of the penthouse will get 10 parking spaces.

Why invest in Como Residences?

Buying a real estate unit in Como Residences is an ideal investment tool. Properties in the project will become among the most desirable on Palm Jumeirah. Living in Como Residences will allow you to experience exclusive beachside living and be provided with concierge services and a whole range of premium amenities.

Como Residences is an ideal option for single professionals and couples wishing to enjoy waterside living with access to lots of entertainment venues. A range of properties with various layouts and amenities located on the territory of the complex, as well as a number of educational and medical institutions within the surrounding areas will be especially appreciated by families with children.

Once completed, you can live in a luxurious gulf-side complex or resell the property at a higher price. The average ROI for a 2-bedroom apartment on the island is currently 4.5%, which means that renting a residence out will provide you with a stable income.

Amenities

A perfect blend of luxury amenities will be offered to occupants of Como Residences, fulfilling every moment of their lives with unforgettable experiences. These include landscape recreational areas, a private sandy beach pool, dry and wet children’s playgrounds, a business centre, a cafe, sports facilities such as a gym, a spa and wellness centre and more to come. 

Furthermore, living in the heart of Palm Jumeirah will enable you to enjoy easy access to some of the iconic leisure destinations in the city. Aquaventure Waterpark offers over 105 rip-roaring slides and attractions, whilst The Lost Chambers Aquarium is the perfect place to learn more about sea life and get acquainted with the marine creatures.

Shops & Supermarkets

Occupants of Como Residences can make quick purchases at QuickBuy Supermarket, allday fresh — Golden Mile and VIVA Supermarket — Palm Jumeirah, located within a 5-minute drive. Within close proximity to Como Residences are several malls, including Nakheel Mall that boasts more than 140 shops, 30 dining options and a range of entertainment activities, including Trampo Extreme and Fabyland. Golden Mile Galleria is another option and is known for its lifestyle stores and dining venues, including Magnolia Bakery, The Yellow Chilli and Habib Beirut. Dubai Marina Mall, which is one of the most popular shopping destinations in the emirate, can be reached within 10 minutes by car.

Hospitals, Schools, Kindergartens

Among the closest kindergartens and schools that are reachable within a 5–15 minute drive, are Asya’s Nursery, Blossom Palm Jumeirah Nursery Dubai, Redwood Montessori Nursery — Golden Mile, iCademy Middle East and Emirates International School — Meadows. Occupants of the development will be able to get professional medical help in Al Das Medical Clinic, Dubai London Clinic — Nakheel Mall and Emirates Hospital Clinics — Palm Jumeirah, which are all located within 5–10 minutes by car.

Transportation

In terms of public transport, Al Ittihad Park Monorail Station will be located within a several-minute walk from Como Residences, enabling occupants to get to Dubai Marina and Palm Jumeirah Tram Stop quickly and efficiently. However, living in Dubai is more convenient if you travel around the emirate via private vehicle. Dubai Marina and the Sheikh Zayed Road are both reachable from the development within just 10 minutes by car. Downtown Dubai and Business Bay will be accessible within 20 minutes, and Dubai International Airport is within a 25-minute drive.
